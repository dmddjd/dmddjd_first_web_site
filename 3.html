-심은성-

Following the report of the Strath Committee in 1955, the British government developed contingency plans for the continuity of government.
This included construction of the underground Central Government War Headquarters (CGWHQ), codenamed "Burlington",
at Corsham in Wiltshire. As planned, it would have been a "reserve Whitehall" where the central government
could be moved in an emergency and, hopefully, survive a nuclear attack.
